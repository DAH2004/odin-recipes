Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta. A classic Italian appetizer. The bread doesn't matter quite so much so long as it is firm. However, the simplicity and short ingredient list means it will help to be choosy with the topping. This is completely optional, of course. As long as the input is in the ballpark, so will be the output.

Ingredients

  • French bread, 1 loaf, cut into 1/4 inch slices
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tbsp & 1 tsp
  • Roma (plum)* tomatoes, diced
  • Fresh* basil, 1/3 cup
  • Parmesan cheese, 1 oz, freshly grated*
  • Garlic, 2 cloves*, minced
  • Good quality* Balsamic vinegar, 1 tbsp
  • Kosher salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Freshly-ground* black pepper, 1/4 tsp

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F / 200 C

  2. Step 2

    Brush bread slices on both sides lightly with 1 tablespoon oil and place on large
    baking sheet. Toast bread until golden, about 5 to 10 minutes, turning halfway through.

  3. Step 3

    Meanwhile, toss together tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl.

  4. Step 4

    Mix in balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.

  5. Step 5

    Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices.

  6. Step 6

    Profit.
