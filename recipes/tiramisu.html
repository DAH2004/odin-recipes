Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu, the classic Italian desert. You know it. You might even love it.
This recipe can be completed without the oven and does not take much time to prepare.
However, serving will have to wait until it is cooled. Consider prepping the night before.

Ingredients

  • Water, 1 cup
  • Instant espresso coffee powder, 2 tbsp, divided
  • Coffee liqueur, such as Mr. Black, 6 tbsp, divided
  • Mascarpone cheese, 2 (8-oz packages), softened
  • Cream cheese, 2( 8-oz) packages , softened
  • Powdered sugar, 1 cup
  • Salt, 1/4 tsp
  • Vanilla extract, 1 tbsp
  • Crisp Italian ladyfingers (savoiardi), 1 (7-oz) pkg , divided
  • Sliced almonds, 1 cup , toasted
  • White sugar, 2 tbsp
  • Butter, 1/3 cup , melted
  • Whipping cream, 2 cups
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting), "as needed"
  • Chocolate covered espresso beans, 12

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil

  2. Step 2

    Pour boiling water into a small bowl and stir in 1 tbsp instant espresso
    coffee powder until dissolved. Set aside and allow to cool to room temperature,
    30 minutes. When cool, add 2 tbsp coffee liqueur. You will use this
    mixture to soak ladyfingers for the filling.

  3. Step 3

    Meanwhile, for the filling beat mascarpone and cream cheese in a large bowl
    with an electric mixer on medium until smooth. Beat in powdered sugar,
    remaining 1 tbsp instant espresso coffee powder, and salt until well
    combined. Beat in vanilla and remaining 4 tbsp coffee liqueur. Cover
    bowl, and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    For crust, combine 12 ladyfingers, almonds, and white sugar in a food processor.
    Pulse to fine crumbs. Remove mixture from food processor. Stir in butter until

  5. Step 5

    Pat crumb mixture evenly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
    Place pan in the freezer until set, 15 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    Beat cream in a large bowl with an electric mixer until it forms stiff peaks.

  7. Step 7

    Stir one fourth of the whipped cream into the filling mixture to lighten. Fold
    remaining whipped cream into the filling mixture until combined.

  8. Step 8

    Spread half of the filling over the crust.

  9. Step 9

    Dip remaining 12 ladyfingers, one at a time, into coffee mixture to coat, about 1
    second. They will soften quickly and break easily. Place atop the filling, breaking
    to fit as needed. Spread remaining filling evenly over the ladyfingers.

  10. Step 10

    Cover and chill in the refrigerator 6 hours to overnight.

  11. Step 11

    Dust cocoa powder generously over top of the cheesecake. Loosen sides of pan with a thin spatula. Remove sides of pan.

  12. Step 12

    Garnish each slice with a chocolate covered espresso bean.

  13. Step 13

    Profit.
